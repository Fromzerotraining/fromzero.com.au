What Is The Combine

NBA Draft Style.
Melbourne Built.

The From Zero Combine is an NBA draft-style assessment designed to find the most elite basketball talent aged 15–17 in Melbourne's north and west.

Using the same technology trusted by professional sports organisations worldwide, we'll put you through a comprehensive physical and on-court evaluation — measuring exactly what separates good players from elite ones.

This isn't just a testing session. The top 10 performers earn a fully paid basketball tour to China — competing internationally and gaining the exposure that changes careers.

Spots are limited. This is your chance to prove where you stand.

The Assessment

What We Test

01
VALD Technology

Force plate testing measuring explosive power, rate of force development, left/right asymmetry and reactive strength — the same technology used by NBA and AFL programs.

02
Vertical Jump

Countermovement jump and approach jump measured with precision — giving you your true vertical and explosive output benchmarked against elite Australian basketball players.

03
Sprint & Agility

Linear speed, acceleration and change of direction assessed under game-realistic conditions — measuring how fast you are, not just how fast you think you are.

04
On-Court Ability

Skills, decision making, basketball IQ and competitive performance assessed by From Zero's elite coaching staff — because the game is where it all comes together.

The Prize

Win a Fully Paid
China Tour

The top 10 players selected from the Combine will earn a fully paid basketball tour to China — flights, accommodation, meals and competition all covered by From Zero.

This isn't a holiday. You'll travel to Shanghai, Beijing and Guangzhou to compete against the top basketball players in China — developing players from the same programs that have produced NBA and CBA professionals.

Three cities. Three different competitive environments. All at the highest level available to players your age. This is the kind of international exposure that changes trajectories — the experience that separates your profile from every other player a college coach sees.
