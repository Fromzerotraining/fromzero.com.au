Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need a referral to see a sports physio?+
No referral needed — you can book directly. If you have private health insurance you may be able to claim on sessions. A GP referral is required for Medicare EPC plans only.
What's the difference between sports physio and regular physio?+
Sports physio is focused on athletes and active people. At From Zero it's also integrated with S&C — your rehab and training work as one system, not in isolation from each other.
Can I see a physio even if I'm not injured?+
Absolutely. Pre-season screening and movement assessments are some of the most valuable things an athlete can do — identifying weaknesses before they become injuries.
Where is the physio located?+
Warehouse 73/2 Thomsons Road, Keilor Park VIC 3042 — same location as our S&C facility. Everything is under one roof.
I've had this injury for months — is it too late?+
No. Chronic injuries often respond well to the right treatment — many athletes have dealt with something for months that resolves in a few targeted sessions. It's never too late to get it properly assessed.
